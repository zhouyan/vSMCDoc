Detailed Description

template<typename T, std::size_t Alignment = 32>
class vsmc::AlignedAllocator< T, Alignment >

Aligned allocator.

This allocator can be used in place of std::allocator. The returned pointer by allocate is aligned with Alignment, which must be power of two and no less than sizeof(void *).
