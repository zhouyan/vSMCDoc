Detailed Description

template<typename RealType = double, typename Left = Closed, typename Right = Open>
class vsmc::UniformRealDistribution< RealType, Left, Right >

Uniform real distribution with variants open/closed variants.

This distribution is almost identical to C++11 std::uniform_real_distribution. But it differs in two important aspects

  • It allows the interval to be either open or closed on both sides.
  • It requires that the uniform random number generator to produce integers on the full range of either std::uint32_t or std::uint64_t.
    Template Parameters
    RealTypeThe floating points type of results
    LeftShall the left side of the interval be Open or Closed
    RightShall the right side of the interval be Open or Closed
