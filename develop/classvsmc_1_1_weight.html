virtual void vsmc::Weight::read_resample_weight ( double *  first) const
inlinevirtual

Read normalized weights through a pointer for the purpose of resampling.

In this class, resample_weight and weight are identical. However, in derived classes they might not be. For example, in WeightMPI, the resample_weight give a vector of normalized weights on all nodes while weight give the weights on the calling node.
